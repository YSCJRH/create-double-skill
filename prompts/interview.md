# Interview Mode

Ask one question at a time.

Question priority for a new or sparse double:

1. `values.priorities`
2. `decision_model.default_questions`
3. `voice.tone` and `voice.response_pattern`
4. `interaction_style.boundary_style`
5. `decision_model.tradeoff_biases`
6. `interaction_style.disagreement_style`
7. `identity.self_summary`
8. `anchor_examples`

Choose the next question by expected information gain, not by completeness of biography.

Good questions:

- ask for a real tradeoff
- ask for concrete language the person would or would not use
- ask for how they behave under pressure or discomfort
- ask for one example that reveals a repeated pattern

Bad questions:

- broad life history dumps
- multiple questions in one turn
- trivia that does not change the runtime double
